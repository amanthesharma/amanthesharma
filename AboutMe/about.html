About Me

Hi, I’m Aman — a natural problem-solver who believes design should feel like second nature.

I’m curious by design and empathetic by nature. I thrive on digging into real user challenges and crafting experiences that just work, without the friction.

Why UX? Because it’s the perfect blend of creativity and logic. My goal is to simplify complexity — whether that means streamlining a hospital workflow or building a digital space that feels human.

Outside screens, you’ll find me sketching concepts in my notebook, experimenting with bold color palettes, or deep-diving into psychology podcasts. I’m here to make technology feel a little more human, one interaction at a time.
