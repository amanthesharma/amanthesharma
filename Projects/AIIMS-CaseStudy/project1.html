SehatSathi

Duration: 3 Months | Role: UX Designer

Project Overview

Problem Overview

“On a hot day at AIIMS Patna, I witnessed the pain of hundreds navigating a public health system with no signage, zero digital touchpoints, and total confusion...”

Project Summary

SehatSathi is a user-centric digital platform aimed at transforming patient and caregiver experiences in public healthcare settings. Leveraging thorough UX research and iterative design, the app provides clear hospital navigation, real-time patient updates, streamlined communication, and AI-supported features.

Problem Statement

How might we improve the hospital experience for patients and caregivers in Indian public hospitals by reducing confusion, improving information access, and offering timely support?

Design Process

  1. Empathize
  2. Define
  3. Ideate
  4. Prototype
  5. Test

Research

Research Goals

  • Understand the main challenges faced by patients, families, and staff.
  • Identify communication and navigation gaps.
  • Determine essential features that would meaningfully improve user experience.

Methods Used

  • Conducted 10 in-depth user interviews (patients, caregivers, hospital staff).
  • Collected over 50 user surveys.
  • Observed hospital processes and patient interactions at AIIMS Patna.
  • Performed competitive analysis of existing healthcare solutions.

Key Findings

  • 75% of users felt overwhelmed by hospital navigation.
  • 65% preferred real-time updates on patient status.
  • Long waiting times due to manual processes increased user frustration.
  • Staff expressed the need for digital tools to manage patient queries effectively.

Personas

Observation Insights

  • Users frequently asked staff for directions due to poor signage.
  • Families experienced anxiety waiting for updates on their loved ones.
  • Manual paperwork and multiple counters caused operational inefficiencies.

Survey Insights

Families Need Updates

Critical to reduce anxiety and confusion.

Manual Processes Stall

Online check-in and notifications improve flow.

Better Signage

Clear wayfinding eases stress and navigation.
