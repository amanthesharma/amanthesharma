SehatSathi

Duration: 3 Months | Role: UX Designer

Project Overview

Context and Motivation

Healthcare accessibility in public hospitals, particularly for underserved communities, remains challenging. My firsthand experience at AIIMS Patna revealed significant issues like poor signage, inadequate communication, and cumbersome manual processes. This inspired SehatSathi—a UX-driven initiative to create a more accessible, efficient, and transparent hospital experience.

Project Summary

SehatSathi is a user-centric digital platform aimed at transforming patient and caregiver experiences in public healthcare settings. Leveraging thorough UX research and iterative design, the app provides clear hospital navigation, real-time patient updates, streamlined communication, and AI-supported features.

Problem Statement

Patients and families at public hospitals face difficulties due to inadequate information, confusing navigation, lack of real-time communication, and burdensome manual processes. These challenges significantly impact patient care and satisfaction, especially during critical emergencies.

Research

Research Goals

  • Understand the main challenges faced by patients, families, and staff.
  • Identify communication and navigation gaps.
  • Determine essential features that would meaningfully improve user experience.

Methods Used

  • Conducted 10 in-depth user interviews (patients, caregivers, hospital staff).
  • Collected over 50 user surveys.
  • Observed hospital processes and patient interactions at AIIMS Patna.
  • Performed competitive analysis of existing healthcare solutions.

Key Findings

  • 75% of users felt overwhelmed by hospital navigation.
  • 65% preferred real-time updates on patient status.
  • Long waiting times due to manual processes increased user frustration.
  • Staff expressed the need for digital tools to manage patient queries effectively.

Personas

Observation Insights

  • Users frequently asked staff for directions due to poor signage.
  • Families experienced anxiety waiting for updates on their loved ones.
  • Manual paperwork and multiple counters caused operational inefficiencies.

Ideation & Solutions

Ideation Process

  • Brainstormed solutions based on research findings.
  • Evaluated feasibility and user impact.
  • Prioritized high-impact, low-effort solutions.

Top Solutions

  1. Real-Time Patient Update Dashboard:** Immediate patient status, medical test results, and treatment information.
  2. Interactive Hospital Navigation:** Easy-to-follow digital maps and clear directions.
  3. Emergency Support Features:** Quick assistance during emergencies.
  4. AI-Powered Chatbot:** Automated support and multilingual assistance.
