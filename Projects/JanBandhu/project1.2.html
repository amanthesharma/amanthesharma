JanBandhu – आपकी नागरिक भागीदारी का डिजिटल साथी

Project Timeline: 4 months

Type: Individual Work

Background: Despite the rise of civic-tech platforms in India, citizens still face poor redressal, broken feedback loops, and a lack of trust in government apps. JanBandhu was designed to bridge that gap with community-first reporting, transparency, and accessible design.

Keywords: UX Research, Interaction Design, Service Design, Accessibility, Gamification

Tools Used: Figma, Miro, ChatGPT, Notion, Illustrator, Whimsical
